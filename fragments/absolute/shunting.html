Shunting

Within the Station Section: Single Line/Double Line — T/806

  • Beyond Advance Starter and up to opposite FSS: T/806 + Shunt Key
  • Beyond FSS (Block Back): T/806 + Shunt Key + PN
  • Behind a Departed Train: Permitted up to FSS, with T/806 + Shunt Key
  • Double Line — In Advance of LSS (Block Forward): T/806 + PN
  • Double Line — In rear of Outermost Facing Points or BSLB/wrong line (Block Back): T/806 + PN
